BREAD



PLAY THE GAME HERE


Let’s take a break from the videos, and dive into an interactive game. The instructions are simple, read the prompt, and make a decision. Where will this game take you? Every prompt and every choice was created by my silly self. I don’t know why I chose to make this game about a bread person, but something about it speaks to me. In the world I created, the player has to make the decisions by themselves. The difference between life and death is a direct result of the player’s choices. Although the images are mostly A.I. Generated, it’s the humanity in the decisions that make the game interesting and intriguing. In this project, I used A.I. in a minor way, as a small enhancement to the game itself. Music would’ve made the game better though. Maybe one day.
