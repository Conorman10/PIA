COMPOSITION





This video represents the intersection between A.I. and Art. The image in the background is A.I. generated, whereas the final audio-reactive composition, and the music itself, were made entirely by myself. The piece represents how music can make any art piece feel more alive. Music is a subject that has not yet been touched by A.I. in a major way, and I hope that we keep it that way.
