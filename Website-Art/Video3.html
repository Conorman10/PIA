BEACH BOYS





In this video, I explain how the Beach Boys and their frontman, Brian Wilson, made the album “Pet Sounds”. I go through their creative process in making the album, and how Wilson’s personal struggles with drugs, love, and addiction inspired their music. Wilson’s journey is an example of the trials & tribulations that take place in people's lives, and how these challenges can result in some of the most beautiful things that this world has to offer.
