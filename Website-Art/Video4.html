VORTEX



This video has less to do with music, and more to do with human movement. In touchDesigner, I created a project that is reactive to mouse movement. As the mouse moves around the screen, a vortex of red & blue particles follows it around. Without human interaction, the piece would stay completely still, and the true potential of the piece would not be realized. After completing the piece, I added another beat I made in the background in order to make the experience as enjoyable as possible.
