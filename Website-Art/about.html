Theme



My exhibition is going to revolve around Music and A.I., and how the two intertwine. My goal is not necessarily for the you all to learn a whole lot about A.I. or music, but to just enjoy the music itself and what people are capable of creating. I want you all to leave my exhibition with a newfound feeling of enjoyment for music and what it means both sonically and visually. I hope you enjoy it!
