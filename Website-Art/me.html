About Me




Growing up in Seattle, I was an avid soccer player. Playing soccer was all I ever wanted to do, and it's all I ever did. It wasn't untill I came to college that I fell in love with Media Arts. Now, making songs and creating videos is all I ever want to do! My goal in life is to become a staple in american media, whether it be through filmmaking or through other forms of media like making music. Until then, I am happy to keep creating media in my own way. As the great Mac Miller once said, "Wherever life takes me, find me with a smile"
